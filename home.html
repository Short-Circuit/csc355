Tunez Music

Home


Other than being a university project for a CSC355 class, Tunez Music is a music website that allows the user to add their favorite songs into a playlist and much more. As it is a work in progress there is much that could be added to the site, but hopefully Tunez Music could someday reach the same level as Spotify, or Apple Music. Below are just a couple of the things that our team believes in:

  • Modern and aesthetically pleasing website design.
  • Tasteful song and artist choices.
  • Flawless and functioning web apps.

We tried to make the website as user-friendly and functionable as possible. Also seeing how there isn't much that you can do yet with the website, that shouldn't be a problem in trying to learn how to use it. Taking a look at Tunez Music, you will see multiple pages... each page will have their own purpose or function. The main page that does a lot of work is the form page. This allows the user to create a playlist which will be created in a database, then the user can add songs into this playlist and someday be able to save this playlist to their account. For any other help, feel free to contact the Tunez team!



At the top of any page you will see a navigation bar with the titles of each page, there you should find a contact page. On this contact page there will be a contact form. Fill the form out and a member of the team will reach out to you within 24 hours.



Remembering that Tunez Music is a school project and not yet finished makes taking the website not too seriously. However, the Tunez Team tried to model the website off of the same model as a few very popular music sites.

A couple of the sites you could compare Tunez Music too:

  • Spotify
  • Apple Music
  • Soundcloud
